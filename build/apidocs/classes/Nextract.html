countStream

(
  • stepKey
  • direction
)

Defined in build/nextract.js:106

Used to count the number of items flowing into or out of a stream step. Most often used before and after a transform task to see the impact of a task.

Parameters:

  • stepKey String

    A unique name for the step (shared by the in and out buckets)

  • direction String

    Valid values are "in" & "out"

Example:

someReadableStream.pipe(transform.countStream('Step1', 'in'))
                       .pipe(transform.Plugins.Core.Filter.greaterThan('age', 30))
                       .pipe(transform.countStream('Step1', 'out'))
